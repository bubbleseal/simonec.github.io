ABOUT THE CENTRE

BB Gymnastics Centre, also known as BetterBounce Gymnastics Centre is a centre that conducts classes related to artistic gymnastics and tumbling for children, teens and adults alike since 1984.

BB Gym's chapter started off in 1984 when coach cum owner Chong Siew Han founded and established the centre, making it Malaysia's first private gymnastics centre. BB Gym classes are aimed at improving a child's self-confidence in taking the initiative and teamwork, total body awareness, general and specific sports basics, and appropriate social behaviours.

Gymnasts like Sumithra Krishnan (93 SEA Games), K.Kavita (Commonwealth Games 98), Chang Siew Ting (Commonwealth Games 98), Chang Zhi Mei and Siti Aishah Jaafar had their advanced basics training in this gym.

Till present day, BB Gym's team of coaches continues to conduct fun and motivating classes to active individuals.
